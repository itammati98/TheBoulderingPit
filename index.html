How to get started?


Introduction

All climbing areas are prottected by padded floors that are thich enough to absorb impact from falls. As with any sport, there are risks to bouldering. Please climb with caution!

Click to read more...

Spotting

Goal is to guide a falling climber and NOT catch them! Follow the climber's torso with your hands and eyes.

Click to read more...

Falling

Sight your landing and try to land on your feet before making impact with other body part. You should land with equal weight on both feet with your knees slightly bent.

Click to read more...

Gym Hazards

Always be aware of climbers on the walls around you. Maintain a safe distance. Avoid cutting closely around sharp corners or underneath overhangs and be aware of walls behind you.
